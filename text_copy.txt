1. Home Planet: Earth is the planet we call home.
2. Solar System Position: Third rock from the sun.
3. Blue Planet: Covered 71% by water and 29% land.
4. Life Support: Only planet known to support life.
5. Atmosphere: Possesses air for breathing.
6. Rotation: Rotates once a day for day and night.
7. Revolution: Orbits the sun in 365.25 days.
8. Natural Satellite: The Moon is Earth's only satellite.
9. Geology: Contains mountains, oceans, and forests.
10. Age: Formed roughly 4.5 billion years ago.
